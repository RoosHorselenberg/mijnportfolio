During a course on Visual Branding, I designed the branding for an imaginary honey company called Honey and Hive. Here I did visual research to design a brand identity. I eventually applied this to several deliverables. Such as the labels on the honey jar, bag design, business cards and Instagram page.

A bit about the brand

  • Who are they?

    Honey is one of nature's richest sources of vitamins, minerals, amino acids and antioxidants. Honey and Hive produces high quality organic honey to help people benefit from the power of honey. The beekeepers at Honey and Hive are very committed to the well-being of the bees. This is because world populations depend on bees to pollinate crops and their beekeepers try very hard to make sure there are enough bees to do this. Their goal is to offer customers the richest tasting honey they have ever tried.

  • Why and How?

    Honey and Hive is convinced of the power of honey and wants to offer people the best quality honey. They are also very conscious about the importance of bees in our world and thus want to do their part. With the proceeds they want to continue expanding their farm to increase the population of bees in the area and thus do their part to preserve nature.

My Task

My task is to design several deliverables reflecting the designed corporate identity of the honey brand Honey and Hive. In doing so, I conducted visual research to best represent the brand.
